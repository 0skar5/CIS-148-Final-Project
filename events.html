The All-Latvian Song Festival has been held since 1873, it takes place every five years, with the Latvian Dance Festival component added in 1948. I've had the opportunity to perform at the 2013 and the most recent 2023 performances. During the festivals, exhibitions of various forms of art, folk craft, orchestra concerts, and there's delicous food everywhere!
